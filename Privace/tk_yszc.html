Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to this game app and related website (hereinafter referred to as the "Game"), which is developed and operated by TENSEN ENTERTAINMENT TECHNOLOGY LIMITED. Protecting user privacy is one of our top priorities. This document explains the types of information that are collected and recorded by the Game and how we use such information.

If you have additional questions or require more information about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email at mailekejiyouxian@163.com.

Log Files

The Game follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log users when they use the game app or website. All hosting providers do this as part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected by log files may include Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

These data are not linked to any personally identifiable information. The purpose of the information is to analyze trends, administer the Game, track users' movement, and gather demographic information.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like many other game apps or websites, the Game uses "cookies". Cookies are used to store information including users' preferences and the pages accessed or visited within the Game. This information is used to optimize the user experience by customizing content based on browser type or other information.

Advertising Partners and Privacy Policies

The Game may contain advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners. You may consult the respective Privacy Policies of these advertising partners for more detailed information about their practices.

Third-party ad servers or ad networks may use technologies such as cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons in their advertisements. These technologies are used to measure advertising effectiveness and/or personalize advertising content. They automatically receive your IP address when such technologies are used.

Please note that TENSEN ENTERTAINMENT TECHNOLOGY LIMITED has no access to or control over cookies or technologies used by third-party advertisers.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

This Privacy Policy applies only to the Game operated by TENSEN ENTERTAINMENT TECHNOLOGY LIMITED and does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of third-party ad servers or websites for more detailed information, including their practices and instructions on how to opt out of certain options.

Children’s Information

The Game does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has provided such information through the Game, please contact us immediately, and we will promptly remove such information from our records.

Online Privacy Policy Only

This Privacy Policy applies only to online activities and is valid for users of the Game with regard to information shared and/or collected through the Game. This policy does not apply to any information collected offline or via channels other than this Game app or website.
